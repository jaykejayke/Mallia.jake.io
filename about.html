ABOUT US
This website is dedicated to creating custom designs on all kinds of clothing using bleach as the medium to create. since 2024 we have been creating designs on our own clothing for everyday use as well as special events such as concerts. We believe in sustainable clothing so we encourage that you give us a piece of clothing you already own for us to bleach onto for custom designs but we also offer plain black clothing of our own for those that do not have clothing they with to be bleached.
